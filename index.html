What is Psycho Pub?

Psycho Pub is a digital pub that offers craft cocktails and alcoholic drinks as collectible NFTs.

The first collection is composed of 80 cocktails with custom atributes and ingredients, all of them different from one another.

The project is created and designed by Gonzalo Rosendo, and is available for purchase on Rarible, where you can get your favourite Psycho Cocktail and own it on the Ethereum Blockchain.
