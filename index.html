What is Psycho Pub?

Psycho Pub is a digital pub that offers a limited NFT collection, created by Gonzalo Rosendo.

It is composed by 100 digital crafted cocktails and alcohol drinks with custom atributes and ingredients, all of them different from one another.

The project is available for purchase on Rarible, where you can get your favourite Psycho Cocktail and owe it on the Ethereum Blockchain.
